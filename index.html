Latest E-Filing Slip or E-Pin Slip *

For E-Filing Slip:

  1. Go to the login page: https://ez.hasil.gov.my/CI/Login.aspx
  2. Navigate to: E-Filing → E-form → E-form Status → click Acknowledgement Receipt
  3. Click "Print Acknowledgement" and download your E-filing slip
  4. Make sure it is the latest year slip
  5. If you don't have tax payable amount in the e-filing slip → kindly provide E-filing slip only. The amount stated should be (TAX PAID IN EXCESS)
  6. If you have tax payable amount in the e-filing slip → kindly provide E-filing slip + Tax receipt payment. The amount stated should be (TAX PAYABLE)

For E-Pin Slip:

  1. If you have not filed any e-filing yet, you can register your Income Tax account through LHDNM official website: https://edaftar.hasil.gov.my/
  2. OR go to Level 4, LHDNM/Tax Office at Lebuh Pantai, Penang and inform the officer you would like to get an E-pin Number for E-filing
Accepted format: PDF (Max size: 10MB)
